FAQ

  • Do I have to book bike online only?

    Yes. You can visit https://www.instabike.in/ and give your requirements and select a bike from our range of fleet at competetive prices.

  • From where do I pick up the bike?<

    You can pick up the bikes from the address mentioned in your invoice copy which you will receive in your mail after the booking is confirmed.

  • When does my order get confirmed?

    Your order gets confirmed as soon as your online rent payment for the bike is successful. In case of any unsuccessful transactions, your money will be credited back to your account :)

  • What are the documents that I need to submit to take the bike?

    You will be required to submit any original ID proof while picking up the bike. Acceptable ID proofs may include Voter ID, Aadhar Card, Passport, Pan Card etc. You will also need to bring your original License proof for verification.

  • What is the cancellation policy?

    Cancellation Charges as mentioned below
    0 -24 Hours before the trip start– 100% of bike rental
    24 hours before trip start – 20% of bike rental

  • Is fuel included in the tariff?

    No. The tariff only includes bike rental. You will be provided with reserve petrol enough to reach the nearest petrol bunk.

  • Can I have the bike delivered to my location?

    We do have Delivery option available for Bangalore, the bike will be delivered to the customers address but after availing service customer should drop the bike on our drop off location & The charges for the delivery is fixed & it is Rs 300.

  • What is the mileage we get on these premium/high end bikes?

    Mileage depends on your usage and the bike condition. However, we have tested and seen around 20 kmpl for high end premium bikes

  • Is there any limit on the number of kilometers that I can ride for a day?

    There is no limit on the kilometers you can ride :)

  • Is there any deposit that I have to pay?

    Yes. You need to pay a refundable security deposit mentioned in the invoice while picking up the bike. The deposit will be refunded back to your account after returning the bike in 7-10 business days.

  • Do you have late return penalties?

    We will accept late return up to 15 minutes from the actual drop off time. Delay of more than 15 minutes will attract next day rent as penalty

  • Is helmet complimentary?

    Yes. We provide 1 helmet with every bike and a second helmet can be provided on request.

  • Do you provide discounts?

    Yes we love providing discounts and coupons! Keep visiting https://www.instabike.in/ to check out our offers from time to time.

  • Do you provide bikes on Monthly basis?

    Yes we do provide bikes on Monthly basis. For more details, contact us at contact@instabike.in.

  • What all cities are you present currently?

    We are operational in Delhi, and Rishikesh and will launch operations in other Tier 1 cities soon.
