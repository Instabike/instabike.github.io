FEE POLICY

  1. Motorcycle needs to be returned at the specified Date/Time as mentioned during booking on the website/invoice copy. Delay of more than 30 mins without intimating the customer care/Field executive would attract penalty of 100/hr plus standard hourly charges.
  2. Customer has to drop the bike at the same location where it was picked up. No requests will be accommodated for drop location change.
  3. In case the customer feels that they will be late for the drop, they should call the field executive or customer care for extending the trip. Extensions are subject to availability.
  4. Pickup date, time and location cannot be changed once a booking is confirmed.
  5. The customer is not authorized to lend the bike to any person without informing Wheelstreet. In such cases the customer needs to verify the documents of the additional rider before starting the trip. Failure to do so would attract a penalty of Rs. 1000.
  6. In the case of loss of a helmet, the customer is liable to pay a fine of Rs. 1000.
  7. In case of minor damages to helmet, the customer is liable to pay Rs.300/helmet and for major damages, customer is liable to pay Rs.500.
  8. Speed limit and charges are applicable as follows:- 70 km/hr for < 200 cc vehicles 100 km/hr for geared for > 200. Charges will be Rs.100 for every default

  1. The rider needs to be 20 years or older to rent a Bike. In case of Superbikes, the minimum age required is 24. Orders will be cancelled with no rent refunded in case the rider is under the stipulated age.
  2. Below mentioned documents need to be verified in original for each rider. Document 2 will be deposited at the time of pickup and returned during drop off. In case of failure to furnish the documents, the order stands cancelled. No rent refund would be issued in such cases.
    1. Document 1 – Driving License
    2. Document 2 – Passport, Election Card, PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, ID Issued by Central or State Govt. (Any one of the above).
  3. Bike Servicing is mandatory after every 1500 Kms of journey. Invoices are to be shared to verify proper service of the bike. In case of failure to do so, the customer is liable to pay a fine calculated by the vendor.
  4. In case of damage to the motorcycle due to accident/mishandling/carelessness, appropriate charges will be calculated by the vendor and the customer is liable to pay the same to the vendor along with the daily rent until the bike is ready for renting again.
  5. In case of theft, the customer is liable to pay in full the market rate of the Motorcycle.
  6. In case of Engine fault or failure the customer needs to contact with vendor/ Field Executive before repair. Original printed invoice is mandatory to claim reimbursement for the same
  7. Motorcycle bookings are subject to availability. Wheelstreet reserves the right to cancel any booking if deemed necessary.
  8. We provide two helmets per motorcycle as complimentary and they have to be returned without any damages during the drop.
  9. A luggage carrier for Avenger & Royal Enfield can be provided on request for Delhi customers who are going for a long trip. No other accessories are provided with the motorcycles. This is not applicable in other cities.
  10. Rider should respect and follow Traffic rules and regulations. All Challans issued due to rider negligence needs to be paid in full to the penalizing authority.
  11. Driving under the influence of Alcohol/Drugs is strictly prohibited. Wheelstreet will not be responsible to compensate for any mishappenings or consequences in such cases. Customer will be liable to pay for all damages to the vendor for the same.
  12. Any kind of cancellation or refund it will take 7-10 days to reflect your bank accounts.
  13. In case customer wants the bike to be delivered to his address, they have to select delivery option else delivery option will not be confirmed from our end. Delivery option is available for only Bangalore customers and the charges for the delivery is fixed & it is Rs 300.

TRIP EXTENSION

Trip extension requests are to be made at least 2 hours before the drop off time. Such requests are to be made to customer care/ Field executive over the phone. As per booking schedule, they may confirm or deny the request depending on the bike availability.

Charges for Trip extension: Weekdays (Mon-Thu) - 1.2x rent for extended duration Weekends (Fri-Sun) - 2x rent for extended duration

  3. No towing charges will be reimbursed by Wheelstreet in case of any failure in the bike.
  4. The rider bears all responsibility for the bike during the rental duration. The bike needs to be returned to its drop-off location and under no circumstances can the customer abandon the bike without dropping it off.
  5. A kit consisting of a spark plug, accelerator wire and a clutch wire will be given at the time of pickup. The customer is liable to pay the charges for the same if they happen to use it during the trip.
  6. The bike should be properly serviced after 1500 kms of journey. Note: Engine oil and air filter should be changed. (Authorised bills for bike service done has to be submitted at the time of drop-off)
  7. Bikes from outside are not allowed in certain areas in Leh-Ladakh and you’ll have to rent a bike from locals. The bike rented from Wheelstreet is to be parked safely at a hotel or a recognised parking space in Leh-Ladakh.
